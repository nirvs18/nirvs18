About Me

About Me

as an Artist

I've always been a traditional and digital artist. I draw portraits, figures, and digital paintings in my free time. I was born in the Philippines and am 20 years old. Digital Art was my first experience at 14. I liked sketching too much not to make it my career. I joined the animation class at Camarines Sur National High School. I studied there for two years before enrolling in the Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology (BISCAST) animation program. I'm in my second year of advanced 3D animation, modelling, and rigging. And I believe Art speaks for itself.
